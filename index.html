1. Overview

Protecting your privacy is important to us. Leafari is currently designed to process and store user data locally on the user's device only.

At this time, Leafari does not require a user account and does not send personal data to external servers.

2. Data We Process

Leafari may process information that you enter directly into the app, such as:

  • journal entries
  • tracking data
  • plant-related notes
  • app settings and preferences
This information is processed solely for the purpose of providing the app’s features.

3. Local Storage Only

All data entered into Leafari is currently stored locally on your device.

At this time:

  • no personal data is transmitted to our servers
  • no personal data is shared with third parties
  • no analytics or advertising SDKs are used
  • no cloud sync is provided
  • no photo scanning or image analysis is performed

4. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to enable the functionality of the app on your own device, including displaying, editing, organizing, and deleting your entries and settings.

5. Data Sharing

Leafari does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Because Leafari currently operates without backend servers, your data remains under your control on your own device.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your content directly within the app at any time.

If you delete the app from your device, the locally stored app data is also removed, subject to your device and backup settings.

7. Children and Minimum Age

Leafari is not intended for children and is not directed to persons under the age of 18.

Users are responsible for ensuring that their use of the app complies with applicable local laws and regulations.

8. Future Changes to Features

Leafari may introduce new features in the future, such as optional cloud services, image-based plant analysis, or other tools that could require different data processing.

If our data practices change, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly, and the “Last updated” date at the top of this page will be revised.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app, legal requirements, or data practices.

We encourage users to review this page periodically.
